How to Make a Garden Salad

Recipe taken from RecipeTinEats

Description

A garden salad works as a great side dish to any fulfilling meal. Fresh is best!

Ingredients

SALAD

  • 1 small head iceberg lettuce or other leafy greens
  • 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tsp parsley or chives

DRESSING

  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp cooking/kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Dressing: Shake dressing in a jar. Taste and adjust - more oil for creamier/less tan, vinegar for more tang, sugar if you want a touch of sweetness.
  2. Toss: Place all salad ingredients in a big bowl. Pour over dressing and toss well.
  3. Serve: Transfer to serving bowl and serve immediately!
